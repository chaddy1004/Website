Junior Hardware Engineer

My Responsibilities

As a junior hardware enineering in Agfa Graphics, I was mainly in charge of creating hardware changes on the printers that are being developed in the factory. The manufacturing factory was directly connected to the office, meaning that I was able to gain hands on experience in assembling, test the changes that I made directly, as well as see the new machines that had my changes implemented. Aside from making design changes and fixing problems, my manager gave me handful of projects which involved making test fixtures for easier testing of certian QA protocols. This job allowed me to gain a lot of hands on experience and look at impact of design from the assembly/manufacturing point of view. Through shadowing a technician, I learned proper methods of cable management, crimping, soldering, and schematic reading.
